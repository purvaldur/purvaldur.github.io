My name is Thorvald, and I am a danish webdesigner and developer.

I am currently studying 'Information Science' at Vejle Technological College. My aim is to close the gap between design and development as I am also currently developing skills in back-end systems and web technology to compliment my existing skills as a webdesigner and in creating enriching experiences. I love the creative process and thought behind designs. I believe that good design results in effective communication and creates an engagement with the audience.

When I am not coding and designing websites, I like to take long walks if the weather allows it, and I like to cook. After all nothing tastes better, than the food you've prepared yourself. When I have completed college here in Vejle, I plan to move to my nation's capital, to study at the IT-University in Copenhagen.
